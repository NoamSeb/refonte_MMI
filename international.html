ARE YOU INTERESTED IN A SEMESTER IN CANADA?


MMI aims to maintain relationships around the world so that students can benefit from them. For students heading towards the creative pathway, the UQAC university will be happy to welcome students to follow English courses. For students aiming to join the development and interactive media pathway, the Cegep will welcome students in order to bring them knowledge related to the web in an English-speaking context.
