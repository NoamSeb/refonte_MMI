INTERNATIONAL

Want to study abroad ?

mmi for everyone

You are a International student who wants to join MMI? Here are the things you need to know.

EUROPEAN STUDENT ?


European students wishing to study in France must follow the same registration procedure as French students. European students wishing to enter French higher education from the first year of a degree program must apply via the "Parcoursup" platform.

INFORMATION

European students are students who have the nationality of one of the countries of the European Union, but also students from Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Switzerland, Monaco or Andorra.

67 COUNTRIES

You can found the list of all the countries here : https://www.campusfrance.org/fr/candidature-procedure-etudes-en-france

IF YOU LIVE IN A COUNTRY COVERED BY THE "STUDY IN FRANCE" PROCEDURE


Students who reside in one of the 67 countries concerned by the "Etudes en France" procedure must make a specific request to register in a higher education institution. The Études en France platform is entirely paperless and allows you to manage all the steps involved in registering in a higher education institution up to the visa application.

APPLY FOR THE FIRST YEAR OF A DEGREE PROGRAM


The procedures for applying for registration in the first year of a degree program vary according to your situation.

You live in a country that is part of the "Study in France" procedure and you have the nationality of the country in which you live. (For example, you live in Morocco and have Moroccan nationality)

If you are preparing a French or European baccalaureate, you must make your wishes on the "Parcoursup" platform and follow the "Studies in France" procedure at the same time.

If you are preparing another diploma (giving access to French higher education), you will have to make a Demande d'Admission Préalable (DAP) and follow the "Etudes en France" procedure. The DAP is done online from the "Etudes en France" platform.

If you live in a country that is part of the "Etudes en France" procedure and you are a citizen of a European Union country, (for example, you live in Senegal but have German nationality), you must only make your wishes on the "Parcoursup" platform.
